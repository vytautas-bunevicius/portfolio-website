About Me
I started out at ISM University of Management and Economics, getting a grip on finance and how businesses tick. Then at Turing College, I dove into data analysis, machine learning, and automation. Now I'm back there as a mentor, helping newbies debug code and figure out the ropes.
At Nord Security, I'm an analyst building tools to wrangle data better. I've set up pipelines pulling from places like Ahrefs and YouTube, built a web scraper to snoop on what people say about us online, and made a model to pick out websites worth partnering with.
I also put together a React and PostgreSQL hub to track API usage, script status, and marketing metrics. It cut errors by 30% and saved the team a bunch of time after I wrestled with some messy state and sync issues.
Before that, I was at CyberCare, figuring out how to explain tech to clients and racking up over 200 new business accounts. It taught me how to talk straight and spot what companies really need.
